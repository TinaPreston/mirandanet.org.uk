The ICT Curriculum


A pupil with Lawrence Williams explaining how she programmed in ScratchA key theme was the ICT curriculum. Notable amongst teachers’ presentations was Literacy from Scratch - a curriculum exchange between Dr Mirka Cernochova from Charles University, working with a school committed to creativity in the Czech Republic and Lawrence Williams, Brunel University, Literacy from Scratch programme, working with Bishop Ramsey, Ruislip, London. The literacy skills project in story telling, uses Scratch, an introductory programming language across the whole of the Year 8 mixed-ability group.

Support materials for other teachers to use are also being written, tested, and developed at the school, and are being sent out for trials in the Czech Republic.


Achieving a World Ecitizens’ certificate for publicationThe pupils who presented the stories they had programmed were awarded a World Ecitizens certificate for sharing their knowledge with others on this website for pupils’ publications.

There is more information about this project on the Literacy from Scratch web site:

  • www.literacyfromscratch.org.uk
