Welcome to MirandaNet at BETT09, 14 -17 January 2009

Register now for BETT 09 to receive your fast track pass to see the World’s Largest Educational Technology event. Registering for BETT has never been easier, simply enter your surname, click ‘login’ and then update your personalised registration form by clicking here.

BETT is free to attend and visiting will grant you access to see cutting edge technology in action, discover future technology trends, their uses and implications, as well as offering a unique networking opportunity.

With new and exciting features being added to our show all the time, don’t forget to regularly visit the WHAT’S ON page to see what BETT can offer you. You might also like to have a look at the electronic version of the show guide New@BETT.

The Seminar programme is now live. Jam-packed with more than 100 practical and inspiring sessions led by teaching practitioners and experts, it covers every subject area and level of education. This year a call for papers was sent out to senior educationalists on the BETT database to create a comprehensive practitioner led programme. Click here to view and book now.

Visit now to see the Training Session programme and Software Session programme to see what companies can offer YOU to help develop your develop ICT skills and keep you up to date with what’s new in educational technology.

Here are a few reasons why you shouldn't miss out on BETT 2009

  • Interact with learners, hear their views on learning of the future and add your voice to theirs in Learning Elsewhere
  • Listen and learn from practitioners demonstrating the very latest products in the New Technologies Zone
  • Detailed seminars concentrating on this years key themes:
    • Personalised Learning
    • Curriculum and Assessment
    • Management and Leadership
    • Modernisation of Schools
  • Participate in best practice presentations within the new Best Practice Theatre

With over 600 exhibitors, BETT is sure to inspire you with lots of invaluable ideas.

Pre-plan your visit at www.bettshow.com
BETT is jam-packed with exhibitors, products, features, seminars and workshops. Handy tips on planning your visit can be found on the website. You can:

  • Search for suppliers and products by key stage, subject or product area
  • Create your own personalised floorplan using MYBETT
  • Choose which seminars to attend
  • Keep up to date with what's happening at the show in the many special zones and feature areas
  • Read articles of interest to you, provided by Schoolzone

Planning an Inset day
If you’re planning an Inset day or would like further information on how to organise one then please follow this link for the essential guide.

We look forward to welcoming you to BETT 2009, 14 to 17 January, Olympia, London.

PS: don't forget to visit Keith's BETT Blog. Keith Clifford, Marketing manager at BETT, will be offering weekly industry updates and behind the scenes information on this years event.
