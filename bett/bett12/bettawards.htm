BETT Awards 2012

BETT12 | BETT Awards

January 11 2012 Hilton Park Lane, London

There are now just a few days to go until the entry deadline for the BETT Awards 2012. Entries have been streaming in from companies wanting to highlight their innovative products and showcase their achievements.

Enter online NOW to ensure that yours feature alongside them!

The BETT Awards are a fantastic opportunity to:

  • Showcase your innovation and excellence
  • Demonstrate to peers, competitors and potential clients that you are at the cutting edge of technology for education
  • Position your product as a market leader in this competitive industry by being shortlisted or winning

Considered by many as the most esteemed accolade in the industry, the BETT Awards provide a showcase of resources and companies that seek to provide educators with the information, ideas and inspiration that are fundamental to the learning process.

The latest research by BESA indicates that:

‘There is an extensive requirement for digital content in a third of schools – up from a quarter in 2010.’

With this is mind it is vital that you are promoting your product to those that matter. A BETT Award is the perfect way to highlight your excellence.

Register online at www.bettawards.com and begin your entry today. Please note, the entry deadline is Friday 30 September.

The BETT Awards Team
