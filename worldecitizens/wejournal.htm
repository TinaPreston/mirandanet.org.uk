World Ecitizens EJournalPeer support for leaders

The World Ecitizens Ejournal is a gateway where the World Ecitizens community leaders and teachers can share ideas for developing humanitarian projects using ICT as a bridge. Leaders who publish in the World Ecitizens Ejournal achieve a Fellowship in World Ecitizenship for using elearning to draw the peoples of the world together. If you would like to participate in world collaborations, have notice of conferences or learn more about World Ecitizens online courses.
