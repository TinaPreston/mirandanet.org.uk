Dominic Preston

Dominic has been involved with the MirandaNet Fellowship since it started. In one of the projects he managed at Hermitage School in Wapping, London, he involved comic artists who developed the pupils narrative skills. The comic that resulted, called Living, was based on the experience of being ‘down and out’ in London.  As part of their citizenship studies the children researched the reasons for the homelessness they saw so often in London. The project was supported  by AD 2000 and Crisis and funded by Creative Partnerships from the Arts Council of England.

Dominic is currently Content & Marketing Director at Audiotube, a music start up that is setting new standards in music video quality and syndicating independent music video across the web and beyond. This directorship brings together ten years experience in consumer marketing covering a range of products ranging from audio technology, computer games and comics.
