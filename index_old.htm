The MirandaNet Fellowship, founded in 1992, is an e-community of practice for international ICT policy makers, teachers, teacher educators, researchers and commercial developers who are passionate about digital technology in teaching and learning and about using technologies to promote cultural understanding and democratic participation. Currently there are around 700 members in about 70 different countries worldwide. The website, online forums, seminars, workshops and projects run by members are funded by international partner companies and government agencies. MirandaNet gratefully acknowledges the support of its company partners.
