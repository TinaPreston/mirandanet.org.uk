About Tablet Academy| David Fuller | Pilot Study

David Fuller is a qualified teacher with over 20 years teaching experience in both state and independent schools. He has taught ICT throughout his teaching career and has expertise in teaching Year 7 through to Year 13. David is also a governor of a primary school and is familiar with the needs of primary school ICT requirements.

David has successfully established and run an Apple RTC (Regional Training Centre) in Cambridgeshire and led the provision of IT training for the staff at Oundle School. David has used Apple Mac computers since the 1980s.

David was nominated for a national teaching award in 2010 and has acted as an examiner and moderator for both GCSE and A Level ICT examinations. He was also involved in writing the new A Level ICT specification.

In the past David has worked with clients such as Shell UK, Michelin UK, Barclays Bank, Lloyds TSB, SP Manweb plc, and many other corporations. He is an expert in developing relationships and providing innovative solutions, as well as providing great after care service.

David is a Chartered Engineer and Chartered IT Professional and a Member of the BCS, The Chartered Institute of IT (formerly the British Computer Society). David has current enhanced CRB clearance.

David is long-standing member of MirandaNet.
