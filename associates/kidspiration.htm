Northumberland class uses InspireData® to measure e-safety amongst its school’s pupils
Learn how pupils at Seahouses Middle School in Northumberland, UK used InspireData(r) to determine the level of e-safety practised by their peers. They conducted a survey to collect data about pupils online habits and analysed the data by creating different plots and charts within InspireData. ICT consultant Steve Robson quotes, "Pupils really enjoyed using InspireData and liked the way they could drill down into their data and graphically see the answers to their questions immediately. The e-safety survey was an important piece of work which placed ICT learning into a real and immediate context".
