Support from MirandaNet Associates

Our associate companies will be supporting a range of events during the year 2014/2015 including BETT15 in London in January, and other events during the year.

We also conduct research on behalf of our associates. MirandaNet’s most recent research for company associates, on behalf of Community Playthings, is about space for play for nursery children.

MirandaNet in 2015

We have organised several networking events that provide platforms for companies to present their research in 2015:

21st/24th January, BETT15 when we will be on the DfE stand and presenting on several panels. We will also be giving two seminars at the LearnLive theatre on 21st Wednesday am followed by a MirandaNet lunch.

24th February Policy ThinkTank event at LKL with the International Baccalaureate organisation, ITTE and IFIP to debate how to introduce young people to the kind of digital wisdom that will enable them to be successful citizens in tomorrow’s constantly evolving digital landscape.

March 25th/26th Naace Conference in Nottingham running ICatalyst research sessions

June 30th/July 1st : Digital Education Show (London)

We are also partnering the London Knowledge Lab, Institute of Education in the 2014/2015 series of What the Research Says.
