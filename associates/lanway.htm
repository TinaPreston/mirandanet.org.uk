Company Partners

IT Excellence from people who understand your needs

Complete IT installations, maintenance & bespoke solutions...
Lanway was formed over 20 years ago on the principle of providing cost-effective IT solutions delivering real benefits, enabling organisations to save money & increase their competitiveness. Whilst the technology & our skills have changed greatly since then, our core values and beliefs have not.

Wealth of Experiences
We have always placed great emphasis on the training & development of our most important asset - our staff. Lanway is one of the UK leaders in maintaining staff skills at the highest levels, ensuring our customers always benefit first from new advances.

Strong industry partnerships
Our success has also been our ability to establish partnerships at the highest level with all the major IT manufacturers. This ensures we are able to provide the latest technologies & support skills.
