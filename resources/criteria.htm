Resources | Links | Literacy Links | References | Websites of the month| MirandaRoses

MirandaRoses is one of our old traditions which we hope to resurrect (perhaps with the help of a sponsor?).

They were awarded to the best educational site of the month and the best community or subsidiary site of the month. They have not been awarded for over a year now (Jan 2006).

In selecting a MirandaRose site, we look for:

  • educationally sound materials
  • a statement of the aims and objectives of the site
  • owner details and an email link to back up authenticity
  • details of the origin of any data or information
  • mention of any quality checks or referencing of the information
  • creation dates - age may or may not matter
  • last updated dates - shows how active a site is
  • clearly marked archival information
  • good design and awareness of readability
  • a good search box - vital on an information-rich site
  • easy and clear navigation

In judging the content, we ask:

  • What learning objectives does the site support?
  • Is it cross-curricular or key-stage-specific?
  • Is it aimed at teachers or students or both?
  • Does the resource appear to be honest and genuine?
  • Is the resource available in another format?
  • Do any of the materials infringe copyright?
  • Is the information well researched?
  • Is the information durable in nature?
  • Is there adequate maintenance of the information content?
