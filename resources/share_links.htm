Resources | Links | Literacy Links | References | Websites of the month | MirandaRoses

Note: Nowadays, most people just type a word or two into Google and see what comes up, so we were wondering about keeping this section. However, we do get several visitors, and even the occasional contact thanking us for these items, so they are still here, but mostly have not been updated for ages.

Please do not contact us to tell us that a particular link is broken, or to suggest new or alternative links!

  • TES Teaching Resources - TES currently has over 61,000 free teaching resources uploaded, reviewed and rated by members of the teaching profession
    http://www.tes.co.uk/teaching-resources
  • eWorkshop - an extensive site (based in Canada), covering literacy and numeracy teaching in Grades 1-6.
    http://www.eworkshop.on.ca
  • The General Teaching Council - provides a unique channel where the independent and influential voice of teachers can be heard.
    http://www.gtce.org.uk
  • Classroom Connect - online teaching community; helps teachers to incorporate Internet projects into everyday curriculum
    http://www.classroom.com
  • digitalbrain - a Portal site designed to be the central control area (or brain) of the digital nervous system for people to develop as they move into an online environment; impressive and has curriculum content developed by teachers for teachers. The interface is very user-friendly and distinctive.
    http://www.digitalbrain.co.uk
  • ePals Classroom Exchange - gives K-12 students and teachers a sense of what every-day life is like in a foreign country
    http://www.epals.com/about/
  • Education Research Forum within the Virtual Teachers Centre
    http://telematics3.ex.ac.uk/ERF/
  • The Educator - online magazine for educators; genuinely helpful and easy to use for many aspects of professional life 
    http://www.the-educator.co.uk
  • Extempo web guides - an interesting way to help people navigate a site or have a personal learning assistant
    http://www.extempo.com/characters/
  • ICT Teachers
    http://www.icteachers.co.uk
  • ICT in Education http://www.ictineducation.org/
    Terry Freeman's excellent and constantly updated resource
  • Kids Domain UK - useful seasonal ideas for teachers; full of animation
    http://www.kidsdomain.com/
  • Lessons for Maths, Science, Geography, History, Modern Languages, Design & Technology, ITC, and Music. English, Religious Studies, Art, Special Needs, Sociology and Business Studies will follow soon.
    http://www.internetforschools.co.uk
  • Lewisham ICT Support Site
  • Lesson Connection - Microsoft help for US teachers to search for lesson plans for local and state curriculums
    http://microsoft.com/education/?ID=LessonPlans
  • Literacy Hour -- a very visual site looking at the components. Lesson plans included.
    http://www.literacyhour.co.uk/
  • New Opportunities Funded Teacher Training from Apple Xemplar
    http://ali.apple.com/
  • Non-Stop Learning Store from Compaq (Compaq has merged with Hewlett Packard - try this link:
    http://www.hppublicsector.co.uk/education/education.html
  • Oracle Millennium Project
  • Parents Information Network - apparently 60% of secondary schools report that parents ask questions about ICT; this site helps answer their queries.
  • PedagoNet - Learning Resources Search Engine
    http://www.pedagonet.com
  • School Master - pity about the sexist name, but this is a good free service for all schools; does quite a few of the things Scoop does
    http://www.schoolmaster.net
  • Spartacus Educational - free resources for the Internet community
    http://www.spartacus.schoolnet.co.uk
  • TAPPED IN - a community of over 6000 K-16 education professionals and 15 education organisations. Membership and private offices for meetings are both free 
    http://www.tappedin.org
  • Teem - Teachers Evaluation Educational Multimedia
    http://www.teem.org.uk/
  • Tele-School Online Tele-Cafe with a brisk trade in GNVQ assignments
    http://www.tele-school.org/
  • Tesco SchoolNet
    http://tesco.schoolnet2000.com/welcome_p.html
