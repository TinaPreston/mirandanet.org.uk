Please do not contact us to tell us that a particular link is broken, or to suggest new or alternative links!

Literacy Hour

These links are designed to give pointers to resources on the World Wide Web to support teachers getting to grips with teaching literacy in general and the National Literacy hour in particular.

  • Literacy Time (at the Virtual Teachers' Centre)
    http://www.vtc.ngfl.gov.uk/resource/literacy/
  • Literacy Hour - a very visual site looking at the components. Lesson plans included.
    http://www.literacyhour.co.uk/
  • CLPE (the Centre for Language in Primary Education)
    http://atschool.eduweb.co.uk/clpe/
  • BBC Learning Station
    http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/home/schools/
  • BBC Guide to Home Learning Some literacy learning games can be found here
    http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/home/parents/
  • BBC Primary English Web Guide
  • National Literacy Association
  • NFER Research into Literacy Hours
  • Parents Guide to Reading at Home

Texts and Grammar

  • A.Word.A.Day - A service that mails out a vocabulary word and its definition to the subscribers everyday
    www.wordsmith.org/awad/
  • Bible Gateway
    bible.gospelcom.net/bible
  • Classics - Links to the full text of 400 Greek and Roman classics in translation
    the-tech.mit.edu/Classics/titles.d.html
  • Electric Editors - Lots of interesting material on editing
    www.ikingston.demon.co.uk/ee/home.htm
  • Elements of Style - The famous Strunk & White
    www.cc.columbia.edu/acis/bartleby/strunk/
  • English Server - Large collection of interesting resources
    english-server.hss.cmu.edu/
  • Grammar notes by Jack Lynch (US rules)
    www.english.upenn.edu/~jlynch/grammar.html
  • Grammar Slammer Downloadable software program (US rules)
  • Guide to Grammar and Writing (from an American University)
    cctc.commnet.edu/HP/pages/darling/grammar.htm
  • The Quotations Page - A personal collection plus a quote of the day
    www.starlingtech.com/quotes/
  • Reader's & Writer's Resource Page - Reading lists and shared enthusiasms
    www.cc.columbia.edu/acis/bartleby/bartlett
  • Roget's Thesaurus - Searchable out of copyright editions
    humanities.uchicago.edu/forms_unrest/ROGET.html
  • Online Writing Community - Arts Council-funded hypertext experiments
    trace.ntu.ac.uk
  • Oxford Text Archive - distributes more than 2500 resources in over 25 different languages for study purposes onlyota.ahds.ac.uk/ota/public/index.shtml
  • Project Gutenberg - the official sites (many mirrors all over the world); vast library of e-texts, mostly public domain; all in plain text format.
    www.gutenberg.net/
  • Shakespeare Resources - links to quantities of others
    www.shakespeare.com
  • Virtual Teachers Centre Literacy Hour
    www.vtc.ngfl.gov.uk/resource/literacy/
  • Word Tips For Microsoft Word users
    wordtips.vitalnews.com
