Cookies

This website only sets session cookies if you log on. We assume that by logging on you have given us permission to set these cookies which, unlike 'normal' cookies, can only be seen by the server and cannot be used to gather any information about you or your browsing habits. We use the cookies to remember your username and membership status and to allow you to access resources on the site. These cookies are deleted when you log off, or if you close the browser (which means that if you leave your computer unattended in a public area after logging in, you are still logged on unless you close the browser).

If you access one of our sound files, the Yahoo Web Player will place a few cookies which store the volume you have set, and which collect some anonymous information (see Yahoo's Privacy Statement for further details).

There are several sections of the site which use WordPress to function. Again, no cookies are set unless you log in to these WordPress installations. There is further information about WordPress Cookies on the WordPress Codex site.
