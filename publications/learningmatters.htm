Primary ICT

TITLE: Primary ICT: Knowledge, Understanding and Practice
AUTHORS: Jane Sharp, John Potter, Jonathan Allen, Avril Loveless
PUBLISHER: Learning Matters
ISBN: 1 903300 07 X, £14.99
http://www.learningmatters.co.uk

This book covers both the subject knowledge and the pedagogy which trainee teachers will require to satisfy the QTS Standards. It describes the ways in which ICT can be used by teachers, and how it relates to the pupils’ National Curriculum, schemes of work and programmes of study.

Key Features:

  • Extracts from the National Curriculum for Initial Teacher Training
  • Classroom case studies
  • Research summaries
  • Self assessment questions

Contents:

 Section 1:
 » Wordprocessing & DTP
 » Spreadsheets / graphs
 » Databases
 » Multimedia
 » Graphics and drawing programs
 Section 2:
 » Lesson planning
 » Assessment and monitoring
 » Progression and differentiation
 » Class management
 Section 3:
 » Special Educational Needs
 » Professional uses of ICT
 » Health, safety, law and ethics
 » Software for schools
