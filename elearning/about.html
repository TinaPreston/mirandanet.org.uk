Transforming The Way We Learn

This website has been built by MirandaNet to support The Department for Education and Skills roadshows project of 2002. It contains summaries and results of research into e-learning. Users can access an overview of the research, individual summaries and, where possible, the sources themselves.

This is very much a snapshot of research published up to April 2002. It is hoped to add to this and provide a growing resource.

Contributors to the project have been The Department for Education and Skills, MirandaNet, Actis Ltd and the University of Huddersfield.

Important Note

No further funding was made available after 2002, and so this site remains a historical item frozen in time! The resources are still worth exploring, but the links are now largely defunct. (Editor)
