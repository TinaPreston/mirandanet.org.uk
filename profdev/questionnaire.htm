Interactive Whiteboards

We are conducting some research into the use of Interactive Whiteboards in schools and hope that as many of you as possible will be able to fill in a return.

There are separate versions of the questionnaire for Pupils and Teachers:

Questionnaire for Pupils

  • Pupils' Questionnaire
  • Cuestionario para los estudiantes (Pupils: Spanish)
  • 学生问卷调查 (Pupils: Chinese)

Questionnaire for Teachers

  • Teachers' Questionnaire
  • Cuestionario para los Maestros (Teachers: Spanish)
  • 教师问卷调查 (Teachers: Chinese)

Hint: if the Chinese characters are too small to be read clearly, change the size of the text in the browser View menu.

Please encourage others to fill in the requested information. All replies will be treated in total confidence.
