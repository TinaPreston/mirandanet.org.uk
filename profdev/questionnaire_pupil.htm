Interactive Whiteboards - Pupils' Questionnaire

Information about you

(*This information is required only so that we can contact you: answers will be treated in confidence)

Instructions

Read each question carefully and mark the option or options you consider most appropriate. You can choose a maximum of three options where indicated.


Computers at Home

1) Who is good at computers within your family?

 

2) Do you have a computer at home?

 

2a) What kind of computer?

 

2b) Which room is it kept in?

 

3) What kinds of computer activities do you most enjoy? (you can mark up to three options)

Computers at School

4) How much time do you use school computers during the week?

 

5) Does your teacher use an Interactive White Board (ACTIVboard)?

 

6) What for? (you can mark up to three options)

 

7) How frequently are they used?

If ‘Never’, thank you for your time. Click here to submit your response.

ACTIVboards in use

8) How are the ACTIVboards used? (Tick all that apply)

 

8a) Do you use them?

 

8b) How do you use the ACTIVboards? (You can mark up to three options

 

9) Where do you sit?

 

10) Is your classroom different since the board was installed?

If yes, how? Please give a brief description of how it is different:

 

11) Where is the board located?

 

12) Can you reach the top of the board?

 

13) Have the boards changed the way you learn?

 

14) What are the changes? Please choose all the options you consider relevant. Then, for each one you chose, put a number in the corresponding box according to its importance, being 1 the most important, 2 the following in importance, and so on, until you have assigned a number to all the options you chose.

 

15) What lessons do you like best using the ACTIVboard? (choose up to 3 options)

 

16) Which activities activities do you like best using the ACTIVboard? (choose up to 3 options)

 

Using the ACTIVvote system

17) Does the board have a voting system?

 

17a) What do you think of the voting system?

 

Lessons learned from using ACTIVboards

18) Imagine that you don’t have an ACTIVboard. Is there anything which you could not learn easily without it?

 

18a) If you answered “yes”, what would be difficult to learn for you? (You can mark one to three options)

 

19) Can you suggest any new kinds of software you would like to improve the ACTIVboard? (You can mark one to three options)

 

20) Can you give me any examples of work that you have done that show how useful the ACTIVboard has been for learning?

 

21) What else would you like to say about the ACTIVboard?
