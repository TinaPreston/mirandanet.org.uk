About World Ecitizens

World Ecitizens
World Ecitizens (www.worldecitizens.net), the charity, has been set up by the Fellows to provide a web space for pupil publication. On this site there are contributions from students all round the world including those who are in hospital. The Peace Room is an activity for all pupils and includes contributions from schools all over the world. Members from many countries have participated in projects: see our International section for more information.
