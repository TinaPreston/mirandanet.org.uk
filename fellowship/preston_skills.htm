Christina's skills include:

Strategic international leadership in CPD research, programme design and evaluation

  • leading in innovation in the fields of research methodology, practice-based learning, continuing professional development, multimodal literacy, digital technologies in teaching and learning and building communities of practice;
  • innovating in the design of practice-based CPD programmes for teachers using digital technologies including establishing multimodal assessment at Masters level in three universities and in China, South Africa and Mexico;
  • designing, developing, maintaining and researching a web-based collaborative knowledge creation and publication environment for policy makers, researchers, teacher educators, senior managers and teachers. Teachers are also encouraged to publish and influence policy;
  • facilitating the international creation and dissemination of knowledge between teachers in informal CPD exchanges using a themed MirandaMod version of an unconference.
  • project and financial management of large, medium and small research projects

Local, national and international research projects

  • publishing research reports, academic journal papers, international, newspaper articles, educational software, book chapters, keynote addresses, conference proceedings papers
  • designing an academic e-journal for teachers
  • designing, directing and implementing research programmes
  • managing teams of teachers, teacher educators and researchers

Teaching, managing, mentoring in CPD programmes

  • course direction, programme design, teaching teachers accreditation and evaluating courses from certificate to masters level face to face and online.

Sustaining professional communities of practice linked with CPD programmes

  • managing a professional organisation with more than 800 members
  • maintaining a global network of colleagues and students more than countries
  • planning, organising and running local, national and international conferences and seminar programmes
  • liaising with industrial partners

Bid writing, evaluation and review

  • writing bid proposals
  • OFSTED inspector on IOE team
  • evaluator of EU programmes
  • peer review of academic papers for key journals
